01 The Artist

The hands behind every Naoberry face.

Naomi Ekhator is a certified bridal artist and the founder of Naoberry Signature. From her studio on Okigwe Road, she has built a reputation for one thing: faces that photograph beautifully at 8am and still turn heads at midnight.

Her signature is a soft, skin-first glam — enhancing what you already have instead of masking it. Brides, celebrants and everyday queens leave the chair looking like themselves, on their very best day.
